ADB: Android Easy Backup From Recovery Mode¶

adb devices # list devices
adb shell # explore file system
adb pull /sdcard/DCIM .   # pull data
adb push <local> <remote> # push data

Recovery mode on Samsung Galaxy S9

  1. Device is powered off

  2. Press and hold the Volume up + Bixby buttons

  3. While continuing to hold Volume up and Bixby buttons, press and hold the Power button until the device vibrates
